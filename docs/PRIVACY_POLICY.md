# Privacy Policy — FullFunnel HubSpot Audit (Free) Chrome Extension

**Effective date:** [DATE] · **Provider:** FullFunnel [legal entity] · **Contact:** [privacy@fullfunnel.co]

This policy explains what the FullFunnel HubSpot Audit (Free) Chrome extension ("the extension") does with data.

## What the extension does
While you are logged into HubSpot, the extension inventories your workflows (and optionally lists, properties, forms, and flow steps) and generates a report you download to your own device. To access the free report, you provide a few contact details once.

## What we collect — and what we don't
- **Contact details you submit (only):** work email, name, job title, company, and "where you heard about us." You enter these in the unlock form, and they are sent to FullFunnel (through our automation platform, n8n) so we can deliver your audit and follow up.
- **We do NOT collect or transmit your HubSpot data.** The audit runs entirely in your browser. The workflow/list/property/form data and the exported report are processed locally and saved to your device. None of it is sent to FullFunnel or any third party.
- **HubSpot session:** the extension reads your already-authenticated HubSpot session to build the audit, locally. It never stores or transmits your HubSpot password, token, or session cookies.
- **Local browser storage:** we store a flag that you've unlocked the tool (and the email you submitted) in your browser so we don't ask again. This stays on your device.

## How we use your contact details
To deliver the audit and to send relevant revenue-operations content and information about FullFunnel services. **Lawful basis: your consent**, given when you submit the form.

## Sharing
We do **not** sell your data. Your contact details are processed by service providers we use to operate (e.g., our automation/CRM tooling) solely to provide the above. We disclose data only if required by law.

## Retention & your rights
We keep your contact details until you ask us to delete them or they're no longer needed. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing [privacy@fullfunnel.co]. We will honor applicable rights (GDPR/CCPA where relevant).

## Security
Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). We limit access to your contact details to those who need it.

We may update this policy; the effective date above will change. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.
